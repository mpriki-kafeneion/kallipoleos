A Bar. A Sound. A Legacy.

ΜΠΡΙΚΙ

Since 1995, Mpriki Kafeneio has been more than just a bar. It’s been a home for free spirits in the heart of Nicosia. For three decades, we’ve poured drinks, hosted unforgettable nights, and built a community around good music, great people, and timeless vibes.It's where strangers become regulars and regulars become family.
🍻
Thank you for 30 unforgettable years. Here's to the memories we've made and the ones still to come.
